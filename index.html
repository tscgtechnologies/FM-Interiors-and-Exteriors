Execution Strategy

From Idea to Finished Interior

We follow an 8-step process to ensure transparency from initial talks to final keys handover.

Step 01

Consultation

First call or meeting at our office to discuss project types, sizes, budgets, and expectations.

Step 02

Requirement Discussion

Detailed conversation covering bedroom numbers, modular kitchen elements, wardrobes, and exterior needs.

Step 03

Site Visit

Our design experts visit your site in Hanamkonda to analyze wall conditions, heights, plumbing lines.

Step 04

Measurement

Accurate laser and tape measurements taken of the site layout to prevent gaps in modular cabinet fittings.

Step 05

2D / 3D Design Concept

We develop and present layout drawings and realistic 3D renderings of the proposed kitchen, hall, or elevations.

Step 06

Material & Finish Selection

Selection of actual acrylic sheets, laminates, veneer tiles, hardware brands, wallpapers, and paints.

Step 07

Final Approval

Sign-off on the dimensions, colors, rendering selections, and final quote estimates before production.

Step 08

Execution Support

Carpentry, glass installations, paint coats, and light mountings are completed, leading to the final handover.
